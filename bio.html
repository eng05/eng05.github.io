Elwin's Biography

Elwin Ng is currently a senior at Tufts University majoring in Economics and Psychology. At Tufts, he serves on the executive board of various student organizations, including the Tufts Economics Society and the Tufts Vietnamese Students Club. During his junior year, he had studied abroad in South Korea and Hong Kong for a semester each and took the opportunity to improve his Chinese and Korean language abilities and explore Asia. He had previously interned in the legal and accounting departments of the IP law firm Fish & Richardson and in the property tactical team of Liberty Mutual Insurance. During his time at Liberty Mutual, he analyzed data of over 3.5 million homeowner policies through building SAS queries and Excel models, and subsequently developed recommendations by interpreting results obtained from flood customer quality and profitability analysis. Elwin hopes to pursue a career in strategy consulting and hopes to utilize his research and analytical skills to make an impact on companies in diverse industries.
